Historian & Educator

I earned a Bachelor of Arts from Middle Tennessee State University in history, with a secondary focus in Classical Studies. For my Master's, I focused on Antebellum American History with a secondary focus on European Medieval History. My current research and focus is on American immigration history and the development of American Government. Click here for more information.

With the knowledge I gained from my schooling and research, I decided I wanted to pass along this knowledge to others. One of the best things is to see the click in my student's eyes when they finally understand the subject matter, often making personal connections to the information. I am certified to teach in Missouri and Tennessee at the high school and middle school levels in Social Studies and English at the high school leve.

Most of my teaching experience has been focused on higher education, teaching history, government, college success, and tutoring. Click here for more information.
